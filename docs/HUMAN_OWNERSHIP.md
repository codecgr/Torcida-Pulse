# Human ownership gate

Torcida Pulse was built during the 2026 TxODDS World Cup hackathon period. The
current official terms require a natural person to own, materially control, and
submit the entry. An automated agent cannot supply or attest a human identity.

Complete every field before public push or either submission:

- Human team leader (legal/display name): **[REQUIRED]**
- Superteam account used for both entries: **[REQUIRED — do not put passwords here]**
- Brazil eligibility confirmed by the participant: **[REQUIRED: yes/date]**
- Age/jurisdiction/organizer exclusions reviewed: **[REQUIRED: yes/date]**
- Material human review performed (features/code/demo/docs): **[REQUIRED: describe]**
- Human-authored final commit hash: **[REQUIRED]**
- TxODDS written data-display permission reference, or synthetic-only decision:
  **[REQUIRED — store no private correspondence in the public repo]**

The participant should make a final material review and commit using their real
Git identity without rewriting or erasing prior agent-authored commits. The
same eligible human account must submit the Consumer entry and Brasil listing.
